Who We Are

GK FOODS is a private agro-based business entity, registered in Uganda located in Busesa Town, Bugweri District which is in the Eastern part of Uganda. It was founded in 2018 and officially registered as a limited company in 2020. GK foods limited processes grains for human consumption.


As grain processors intended for human consumption, we handle every grain with utmost care and attention through quality assurance standards and procedures. The grains processed include Rice, Maize ,Beans, Millet, Sorghum, Groundnuts, and Soya beans.


Did You Know...?

GKFOODS is a private agro business entity, registered in Uganda and locally owned . We are a diversified business with more than 8 products in our company portfolio. We own our own own mills, pre-cleaning, grading and packaging machinery to enable us offer high quality products to our clients.


What Drives Us

At GKFOODS, we are passionate about doubling small holder farmer incomes by undertaking the complementary farmer capacity building activities,Industry standard processing and Improved seed proliferation

OUR MISSION

" To be the leading provider of essential daily food needs in Uganda through the operation of a profitable and thriving agro-processing business ,thereby improving the living conditions of farmers from whom we source the raw materials."

NURTURING A VISION

GKFOODS continues to grow its vision of becoming the leading provider of essential needs in food, in Uganda. We continue to sustain and improve our food products in Rice, Maize, Beans, Groundnuts, and Soya beans. In line with our philosophy, we will continue to provide the basic food needs of Uganda.
